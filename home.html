What is Abortion?

An abortion is a medical procedure to end a pregnancy. There are many reasons as to why a mother might end their pregnancy. They might not be able to financially support a child. The pregnancy might be a result of rape. The mother might not be ready for a baby. True; having children when you are young sounds very fun and cool.
